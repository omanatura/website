### Cancellation Policy

**Last Updated:** 3/12/2025

At OMA Natura, we strive to provide flexibility and transparency for our customers. Please review our cancellation policy below to understand the terms and conditions for cancellations and refunds.

---

#### 1. **Reservations Made Through Website, WhatsApp, or Email**
- **Regular Tours**: Cancellations are free of charge, and you will receive a full refund.
- **Packages**:
  - Reservations require a **25% deposit** of the total cost.
  - Full payment must be made **at least 48 hours before the trip**.
  - **Cancellation Penalties**:
    - If canceled **more than 48 hours before the trip**: Full refund (excluding the deposit).
    - If canceled **within 48 hours before the trip**: A penalty of **50% of the total cost** will be applied.
    - **No-show or cancellation after the start of the tour/package**: A penalty of **100% of the total cost** will be applied.

---

#### 3. **Refund Process**
- Refunds will be processed within **7-10 business days** after the cancellation request is approved.
- Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during the reservation.

---

#### 4. **Exceptions**
- In case of unforeseen circumstances (e.g., natural disasters, extreme weather conditions), we will work with you to reschedule your tour or package at no additional cost. If rescheduling is not possible, a full refund will be provided.
