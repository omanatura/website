### About this tour

During the tour, the forest comes alive with natural sounds and shifting shadows, creating a vibrant, mysterious atmosphere. Listen to the soft croaks of frogs echoing in the dim light and spot caimans with gleaming eyes guarding the lake. Along the way, you’ll encounter a stunning variety of species, from the iconic red-eyed tree frog to amphibians, reptiles, mammals, birds, and insects, all playing a vital role in the delicate balance of this ecosystem.

Our passionate guides will share insights into the forest’s regeneration and conservation efforts, highlighting how each species contributes to its resilience. This family-friendly experience is both safe and educational, offering a deep connection with nature and a chance to reflect on the importance of protecting our planet for future generations.

The jungle is made up of two forests—explore the one that awakens at night. Join us for an unforgettable journey that will transform your perspective and leave you inspired to care for the Earth.
